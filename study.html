BNY MELON
Financial Product Design • Publicis Sapient

Pershing X required a total data & design overhaul towards unifying their financial product ecosystem.

Through a collaborative multi-track effort, we partnered with the technology teams at Pershing X to deep dive into their product ecosystem with a focus on data architecture and branded design systems. The work wasn't wow-worthy. The client wasn't expecting spectacle. They wanted smart, carefully considered actionable deliverables to propel them forward.

Tech stack migration, data refactorization, & design systems.

Here, I led product discovery sessions, UX and Data Architecture workshops, as well as design-system integrations leveraging React.js and Tailwind.
