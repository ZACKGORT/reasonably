reasonably.

to a moderate or acceptable degree: fairly; quite.

A tool for operating within limits — tracking the nodes through which you work, so you never lose your place.

  • Tap a card to edit it
  • Long-press / right-click for quick actions
  • + button adds a new node
  • Provider pills filter the view
  • Status chip cycles through states
  • Canvas view lets you drag cards freely
  • Ctrl/Cmd + N opens note while editing
  • Active — in use, timer counting up
  • Ready — available, reset complete
  • Cooldown — hit limit, counting down to reset
  • Burnt — fully spent, needs manual restore
  • Dark — deep indigo, glass surfaces
  • Light — warm off-white, refined
  • Rose — deep plum with soft pinks
